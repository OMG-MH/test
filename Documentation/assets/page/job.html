      Job Management

      Job Control Hub

      The Job Management feature in TonaJob equips administrators with powerful tools to oversee and manage all job-related activities on the platform. From reviewing job postings to resolving disputes, this module ensures that jobs are processed efficiently and disputes are settled fairly. This section provides a comprehensive guide to the job management system, detailing its features and functionalities.

      • Job Overview and Filtering
        Admins can access a complete list of jobs on the platform, categorized by their statuses.
        All Jobs : Displays every job posted on the platform.
        Pending Jobs : Lists jobs awaiting admin review for approval or rejection.
        Approved Jobs : Shows jobs that have been approved and are available for freelancers.
        Rejected Jobs : Contains jobs that did not meet platform standards.
        Job Search: Admins can search for specific jobs using various filters like Job code, category, name, author username etc.

      • Detailed Job Information
        For each job, the admin can access comprehensive details.
        Job Author : Name of the user who posted the job.
        Quantity : Number of items or services requested.
        Cost per Quantity : Payment per unit of the job.
        Total Budget : Overall budget allocated for the job.
        Vacancy : Number of freelancers required.
        Job Proof Requirement : Indicates whether proof of completion is required from freelancers.
        Job Details : A detailed description provided by the job author.
        Initiated Date : Date when the job was posted.
        Requirement Document : Any additional documents uploaded by the job author.

      • Job Actions
        Approve Jobs : Publish jobs that meet platform guidelines.
        Reject Jobs : Decline jobs that do not adhere to standards, with or without providing feedback.

      • Applicant and Freelancer Management
        View Applicants : See the list of users who applied for the job.
        Monitor Hired Freelancers : Track freelancers hired for the job.
        Resolve Disputes : Address and resolve issues between buyers and freelancers.

      • Dispute Resolution
        Access Conversations : View and participate in discussions between the buyer and freelancer to understand the issue.
        Download Shared Files : Retrieve files exchanged during the project for better dispute analysis.
        Allocate Funds : Divide the total budget between the buyer and freelancer based on the dispute resolution outcome. Admins can assign specific amounts to each party and finalize the transaction.

      The Job Management module in TonaJob ensures that every job on the platform is handled with utmost transparency and efficiency. With tools for monitoring, reviewing, and resolving disputes, this feature empowers admins to maintain the platform's integrity and provide a seamless experience for both buyers and freelancers. It remains a trusted and efficient platform for micro job freelancing.
