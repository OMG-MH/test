      Deposit Management

      Elevating Deposit Oversight

      As an integral part of the TonaJob, the Deposit Management Module empowers administrators like you to oversee and optimize the process of deposits by users. This comprehensive guide is designed to equip you with the necessary tools and insights to efficiently manage deposit system.

      • Deposit Summary :
        Enables admins to monitor the progress of deposit processing.
        Provides a concise summary of deposit activity, categorizing deposits into successful, pending, and cancelled statuses.
      • Payment Gateways :
        Allows users to deposit through both automated payment and manual payment methods, providing flexibility.
        Ensures accessibility for users who may prefer alternative payment methods beyond automated gateways.
      • Deposit Information :
        Displays information about the payment gateway used for each deposit, ensuring transparency in transaction processing.
        Records the timestamp of each deposit, enabling admins to track deposit activity over time.
        Provides details about donors, including their name, email address, and any additional information provided
      • Comprehensive Search Capability :
        Allows admins to quickly locate specific deposits by entering the transaction number associated with each transaction.
        Enables admins to search for deposits associated with a specific user by entering their username.
        Allows admins to filter deposits based on a specified date range, such as a specific day, week, month, or custom range.

      By mastering the features outlined in this guide, you're equipped to effectively manage deposits on the TonaJob platform. Whether you're monitoring deposit summaries or analyzing payment gateway performance, your efforts are instrumental in ensuring transparency, accountability, and efficiency in the deposit process.
